FAQ

Everything here is focused on the service itself, so customers can get the basics quickly before placing an order.

How safe is Buni Boosting?
Safety is a core part of the service. We do not use bots, we do not DDOS, we do not win trade, and we have completed over 200 boosts with 0 bans.
How long will my boost take?
The calculator estimates delivery time from the rank path, MMR target, or tournament package you choose. Duo orders take longer and the site adjusts that automatically.
How does duo boosting work?
Duo boosting requires a region selection. Standard duo costs more than solo, and tournament duo uses a separate premium because it follows different rules.
How do rewards work?
Rewards use one smart toggle. If the order reaches Grand Champion, the site adds GC rewards automatically. If the order reaches SSL, it switches to SSL rewards automatically.
Do you use bots, DDOS, or win trading?
No. Orders are completed by real players only. The service does not use bots, DDOS attacks, or fake match manipulation.
Can I order SSL MMR instead of a normal rank boost?
Yes. On supported modes, if your current rank is already SSL, the calculator switches into an MMR flow so the order can be priced by MMR gain instead of rank tiers.
Do tournament boosts follow the same pricing as ranked boosts?
No. Tournament wins use their own pricing ladder and delivery times. Duo tournament orders also use a separate duo premium.
Where should I send customers after they build an order?
Use the Discord page as the handoff page. It keeps the same branding and gives you a cleaner next step than dropping users straight into an unstyled invite flow.
