Pricing breakdown

Standard boosting uses a progressive tier path, SSL pushes use MMR-based pricing, tournament wins use a separate ladder, and duo pricing adjusts by region.

01

Tier-by-tier pricing

Standard orders add up every rank tier crossed from your current rank to your target rank. Bronze 1 to SSL totals exactly $100, while GC1 to SSL totals exactly $40.

02

Mode and duo rules

  • 1v1, 4v4, heatseeker, and rumble cost 25% extra.
  • Standard duo is +20% in NA and +30% outside NA.
  • Tournament duo is +50% in NA and +100% outside NA.
03

Rewards and delivery

Rewards are detected automatically from your target when enabled. Delivery estimates scale by rank band, MMR gain, or tournament package, and duo doubles the expected time.
